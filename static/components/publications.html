Hindav Deshmukh, et al.

International Journal of Advanced Research in Science, Communication and Technology (IJARSCT)

April 2024   |   DOI: 10.48175/IJARSCT-16968

Deep Learning Computer Vision OpenCV CNN

Developed a Computer Vision system that identifies movie characters from images and videos using Deep Learning algorithms. This research focuses on utilizing Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for accurate character detection and recognition in dynamic media environments.
